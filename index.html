About me

As someone who has his entire life, lived and being raised abroad, I was given the chance to experience what it was like to grow up in a multicultural environment. This allowed me to grasps intercultural communication at a better understanding which promotes open-mindedness and an overall sensitivity concerning global problematics.

I have always yearned for knowledge, curious in nature, I enjoy talks and discussions regarding multiple topics of activity which directly translates into my willingness to discover and work for different companies specialising in different expertise. But one common aspect that I have found in my past professional experiences, corresponds to the international aspects of the business. This is in fact, the reason why I joined KEDGE.

Space and Defense Industry Procurement

Under the umbrella of ADF Group, I was given the opportunity to take charge of the procurement process of raw materials and needed machineries for the production and fabrication of important international projects such as the European Space Agency's Ariane 6 launch system.

Châteauneuf-du-Pape AOC - Luxury Wine Export

Assigned to conduct a six months market survey for the markets of Mainland China and Hong Kong where the goal is to implement a brand-building strategy for the wines from the Chateauneuf-du-Pape area, registered under the trademark name: “LA GRANDE COLLECTION DES PAPES”.

Luxury Real Estate Management

Under the supervision of the director of the luxury real estate company "Thadée Patrimoine", I had the possibility to research, source and promote incredible estates ranging from villas to mansions at colossal budgets in the French and international estate market.
